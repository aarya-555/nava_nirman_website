About us

Where traditional earth meets modern engineering.

Nava Nirman manufactures precision-made mud interlock and compressed earth blocks, produced to consistent engineering standards so every block performs the same, build after build.

We bring modern manufacturing discipline to time-tested earthen materials, delivering construction that goes up faster, costs less to build and stands the test of time, for homes, commercial projects and boundary walls across Dakshina Kannada and beyond.

Vision

To lead India's shift to sustainable construction, proving that the strongest, most enduring buildings can also be the most responsible.

Mission

To deliver durable, cost-effective building solutions through quality manufacturing and engineering innovation, without compromising on sustainability.

Manufacturing

Pressed, cured and quality-checked.

Every block is produced in-house on a hydraulic press, then cured and stacked, so the strength and finish stay consistent from the first block to the last.

On the press
01

Earth & material mix

Locally sourced soil is prepared and proportioned for strength and consistency.

02

Hydraulic compression

The mix is pressed under high pressure into precise interlocking blocks with their tongue-and-groove profile.

03

Curing & drying

Fresh blocks are cured and dried to develop their full strength before dispatch.

04

Stacked & checked

Finished blocks are stacked and checked for shape and finish, ready for site.

On site

From foundation to finished wall.

See the blocks at work in real builds across the region, homes, boundary walls and compounds, going up faster with less on-site labour.

Step 01 · Foundation

A solid base, course by course

Walls start on a levelled stone-and-plinth base, with the first interlocking courses aligned edge to edge, no mortar bed needed between blocks.

Step 02 · Walls rise

Block by block, storey by storey

The blocks stack and lock into place quickly, letting walls and full storeys go up with a clean, ready face that needs little or no plastering.

Step 03 · Standing strong

Built to face the elements

Completed walls stand full-height and exposed, durable boundary and compound walls finished without cement plastering.
